Building Better Communities Together

Empowering lives through education, healthcare, and sustainable development across India.

InAmigos Foundation works at the grassroots level to create meaningful change in underserved communities. Through collaborative efforts with local partners, volunteers, and beneficiaries, we address critical needs and build pathways to self-reliance.

About InAmigos Foundation

Our Mission

To empower marginalized communities by providing access to quality education, healthcare services, and sustainable livelihood opportunities, fostering dignity and self-reliance.

Our Vision

A society where every individual, regardless of socio-economic background, has equal opportunities to lead a healthy, educated, and fulfilling life.

Our Story

Founded in 2015, InAmigos Foundation began with a small group of dedicated volunteers committed to addressing educational inequalities in rural communities. Over the years, we have expanded our reach to cover healthcare, women empowerment, and community infrastructure development, creating lasting impact across multiple states in India.

Our Projects

Creating sustainable change through targeted initiatives

Digital Education Initiative

Bridging the digital divide by providing tablets, internet connectivity, and digital learning resources to students in rural schools. Our program includes teacher training, curriculum development, and ongoing technical support to ensure sustainable integration of technology in education.

3,500 Students Enrolled
30 Schools Equipped

Mobile Healthcare Services

Bringing quality healthcare to remote villages through fully-equipped mobile clinics. Our medical teams provide free consultations, essential medicines, diagnostic services, and health education. We focus on preventive care, maternal health, and chronic disease management.

8,000 Patients Treated
45 Villages Covered

Women Empowerment Program

Empowering women through vocational training in tailoring, handicrafts, digital literacy, and entrepreneurship. Our comprehensive program includes skill development, business mentoring, access to microfinance, and market linkages to help women achieve economic independence.

750 Women Trained
120 Businesses Started

Community Infrastructure Development

Building essential infrastructure in underserved communities including schools, sanitation facilities, and solar power installations. Our projects are designed in collaboration with local communities to ensure relevance, sustainability, and community ownership from the ground up.

25 Projects Completed
12,000 Lives Improved
